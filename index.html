About

I am research intern in the Snyder lab at Stanford University. I am interested in chronic diseases, nutrition, and quantifying health. In my current research I investigate the sensor technologies of glucose measuring devices, as well as their data policies and practical applications. I did my undergraduate at Rutgers in the honors college with degrees in engineering and physics. I completed my master's degree at Northwestern in material science.
